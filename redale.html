RED ALE BEER

Belgian Witbier

An extremely popular beer style that almost faded away in the 1940s, partially because of the Lager revolution and World Wars. The style was revived in the 1960s and 1970s, and many versions of the beer are brewed in various parts of the world today.

The Tint-In-Wit is our refreshing, Belgian-style fruity ale and our most popular beer. It is made from Pilsner malt and locally sourced unmalted Indian wheat. A classic Belgian Wit with a tint in it.

This beer is also infused with local coriander and fresh orange peel, giving it a spicy-sweet, citrusy taste, making it not only refreshing but also a very easy and sessionable beer.

ABV

4.6%

Appearance

Very pale straw to very light gold in color. The beer is very cloudy from starch haze and/or yeast, which gives it a milky, whitish-yellow appearance.

Mouthfeel

Medium-light to medium body, having a smooth and light creaminess.

Flavour

Pleasant sweetness and a zesty, orange-citrusy fruitiness and hint of coriander.

Aroma

Moderate coriander aroma, zesty, orangey fruitiness.
