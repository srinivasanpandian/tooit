WE'VE BEEN SENDING IT SINCE 2010

We set out with a simple vision of creating world-class craft beer culture. With the promise of some excellent brews, fabulous food, and an inclusive brewpub experience.

Starting out on 100ft Road in Bangalore, we made our way to Mumbai and Pune, setting up breweries in Sanaswadi (Maharashtra) and Nelamangala (Karnataka), and a cidery in Himachal Pradesh along the way.

We decided to start bottling our beloved Tint-In-Wit and our Nitro Stout, India's first nitrogenated beer. We've been sending the bottled brews out (well, in Karnataka for now) to share that extension of the Toit experience with craft beer lovers.

And we're dipping our toe into the cider pool too, with cans of Stray Apple Craft Cider from our cidery in Himachal Pradesh.

For over a decade, we've created neighbourhood spaces (and now pieces of that space in a bottle or can) where you can unwind and be yourself – whether you're there to celebrate, lift your spirits, or just get tight!

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

What makes our beer differnt?

Apart from the essential four (hops, water, yeast, and malt), it contains one more ingredient that’s just as essential to us: our beer has heart. Since our inception, we have been actively involved with a variety of NGOs and charitable organisations with the hope to continue helping them in their journey of making this world a better and brighter place.

We also partner with organisations that support athletes and use the spirit of sporting events to support various causes. We’ve had the opportunity to support organisations that work across education and outreach initiatives, music programmes, livelihood and skills development, animal rescue and rehabilitation, and a host of other areas.

Year on year, these acts of giving back continue to be possible with the support of our patrons and organisations like these. A big Team Toit thank you!
